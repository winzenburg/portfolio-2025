**The Maintenance Trap**

**The Governance Gridlock**
Without clear ownership, design systems turn into political battlegrounds. Debates about button radii drag on for hours because there's no data-driven decision framework. Inconsistencies creep in, and the system fragments as teams interpret guidelines differently.
